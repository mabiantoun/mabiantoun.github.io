Weekly Effort Form Help

Course Homework

Assignment1: Enter the total number of hours per week working on class assignments. Enter each assignment separately.

Assignment2: Enter the total number of hours per week working on class assignments. Enter each assignment separately.

Course Readings: Enter the total number of hours per week spent on assigned readings.

Training/Learning/Client Interaction

Tool Training/Learning: Enter the total number of hours per week spent learning about and training with various tools.

Client Interactions: Enter the total number of hours spent interacting with your client(s) during meetings, or using other forms of communication (e.g., email, chat phone, etc.) to understand and define the requirements, evaluate the various prototypes, etc.

Application research: Enter the total numbers of hours per week spent researching the project application domain.

Team Interactions

Team Interactions: Enter the total number of hours per week spent in meetings or other interactions (email, phone, chat, etc.) with any other team members. Do not include pair-programming.

Pair Programming: Enter the total number of hours per week spent on pair programming. If you spent two hours with a team member pair programming, then both you and your team member should enter 2 for this activity, and not 1 each.

Other

Other Project Work:Enter the total number of hours spent on the project (not one of the above categories): e.g., reading and reviewing other team members’ products, preparing for class presentations, etc.
